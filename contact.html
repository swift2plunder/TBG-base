About the Moderator

To Boldly Go is administered and moderated by Chris Babcock, who administers the USAK Diplomacy Judge. Chris has moderated over 500 games of Diplomacy since 2008.

About the Beta Test

The new game of TBG is in public beta. You are welcome to join and play, but please be advised that there are bugs in the program and incomplete features. Additionally, the beta test will end after ~100 turns. The game will be reset and a new game will run in its place. Please read the rules then click on "Registration" to join.
